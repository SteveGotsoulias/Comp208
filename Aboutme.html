About Me

Teaching Portfolio

Hello! I'm Steven Gotsoulias, a dedicated educator with a passion for inspiring and guiding students in their academic and personal growth. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Education (Secondary) and am fortunate to be a part of the teaching community at Marrickville High School. This portfolio aims to showcase my teaching philosophy, experiences, and accomplishments.

Teaching Philosophy:

My teaching philosophy centers around creating a student-centered and inclusive learning environment. I believe that every student has unique strengths and learning styles, and it's my responsibility to tailor my teaching approach to meet their individual needs. I emphasize active engagement, critical thinking, and fostering a supportive classroom community where students feel valued and encouraged to express themselves.

Please feel free to search the website on my teaching philosophy, my approach in the classroom and my achivements.
