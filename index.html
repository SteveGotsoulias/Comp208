Preperation

Preparation involves activities associated with determining learning outcomes (objectives), researching the topic, lesson planning, and selection of delivery methodologies.

Delivery

Using a variety of delivery methods as appropriate enhances the potential for learners to be successful in meeting the objectives.

Evaluation

Evaluation is the process of determining that learners have achieved the learning outcomes of the lesson.
